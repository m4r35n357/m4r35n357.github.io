base8

Description:

protected void base8 (double s)

General composition from 6th order to 8th order. Performs the following calls per iteration:

base6(s * xOuter)
...
base6(s * xCentral)
...
base6(s * xOuter)

where xOuter = 1 / (4 - 4**(1/7)), and xCentral = 1 - 4 * xOuter
