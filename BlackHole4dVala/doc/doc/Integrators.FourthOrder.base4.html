base4

Description:

protected void base4 (double s)

General composition from 2nd order to 4th order. Performs the following calls to SecondOrder.base2 per iteration:

base2(s * zOuter)
...
base2(s * zCentral)
...
base2(s * zOuter)

where zOuter = 1 / (4 - 4**(1/3)), and zCentral = 1 - 4 * zOuter
