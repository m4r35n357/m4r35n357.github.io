base4

Description:

protected void base4 (double s)

Yoshida composition from 2nd order to 4th order. Performs the following calls to SecondOrder.base2 per iteration:

base2(s * z1)
base2(s * z2)
base2(s * z1)

where z1 = 1 / (2 - 2**(1/3)), and z2 = 1 - 2 * z1
