base8

Description:

protected void base8 (double s)

Suzuki composition from 6th order to 8th order. Performs the following calls per iteration:

base6(s * x1)
base6(s * x1)
base6(s * x3)
base6(s * x1)
base6(s * x1)

where x1 = 1 / (4 - 4**(1/7)), and x3 = 1 - 4 * x1
